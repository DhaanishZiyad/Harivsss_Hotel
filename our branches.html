Our Branches

Colombo

Nestled in the heart of this vibrant city, our hotel offers luxurious accommodations, impeccable service, and stunning views of Colombo's skyline. Whether you're here for business or leisure, you'll find our well-appointed rooms, world-class dining, and convenient access to key attractions make for an unforgettable stay. Discover comfort and sophistication in the heart of Colombo.

Galle

Discover tranquility and charm at our hotel in Galle. Located in the historic city of Galle, our hotel offers a serene escape within the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort. Immerse yourself in colonial architecture, beautiful beaches, and cultural heritage. Our boutique hotel provides a blend of modern comfort and old-world charm, making it the perfect base to explore Galle's rich history and coastal beauty.

Kandy

Nestled amidst lush greenery and rolling hills, our hotel in Kandy offers a peaceful retreat with easy access to the city's iconic attractions. Enjoy breathtaking views, comfortable accommodations, and warm hospitality. Whether you're here to explore the Temple of the Tooth, wander the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens, or simply relax, our Kandy hotel promises an unforgettable stay in this enchanting city.
